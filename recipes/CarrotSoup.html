Carrot Soup

Description

Warm, hearty and healthy. This carrot soup is easy to make, affordable and serves 4

Ingredients

  • 1kg Carrots
  • 1.5l beef/chicken/vegetable stock
  • 3 Cloves garlic
  • Salt & Pepper (to taste)
  • 1 tsp Cumin seeds/powder

Method

  1. Peel and cut carrots into 1" cubes
  2. Tumble carrot with cooking oil, cumin and garlic
  3. Cook in oven at 200 celcius for 45 minutes
  4. Bring 1.5L of stock to a boil
  5. Once cooked, Place carrots in boiling stock and simmer for 30 minutes
  6. Blend
  7. Enjoy
