Pasta Cheese

Description

Simple, affordable and tasty pasta with cheese recipe

Can be made within 10 minutes for those times you need food quick!

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice (200g)
  • Cheddar cheese 50g
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Method

  1. Bring 1.5L of water to a boil
  2. Add pasta and set timer for specified time as indicated on packaging
  3. Grate Cheddar cheese
  4. When pasta seems ready, drain until excess water is removed
  5. Sprinkle on grated cheese and stir whilst hot to aid in the melting
  6. Eat hot - Enjoy
