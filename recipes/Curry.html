Thai Green Curry

Description

One of the most famous curries in the World and a staple for the people of Thailand - Thai Green Curry

Healthy, spicy and full of protein and other beneficial nutrients. A must try!

Ingredients

  • Coconut Milk (400g)
  • 250g Chicken
  • One serving of green curry paste
  • Two carrots
  • Two medium-sized potatoes
  • One head of broccoli

Method

  1. Cut the chicken into cubes
  2. Peel both the carrots and potatoes into cubes
  3. Cut the broccoli head into individual florets
  4. Saute 50g green curry paste in one tablespoon of neutral cooking oil
  5. Add the 400g coconut milk and bring to a boil
  6. Add cubed potato, chicken, carrot and reduce heat to a simmer
  7. simmer until sauce thickens and add broccoli in the final 5 minutes of cooking
  8. Serve with rice - Enjoy!
